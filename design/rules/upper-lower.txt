    there is an exercise-slot s2 with day-order order2 and exercise-order 1 and unassigned primary-muscle-group AND
    the day with order order1 is an upper day AND
    the day with order order2 is an upper day AND
    order1 is less than order2 AND
    there is a muscle-group mg with region f AND
    the user's preferred muscle group is mg does not belong to the upper region

    assign back as the primary-muscle-group of s2

; Assign alternating muscle
IF there is an exercise slot s1 with exercise-order ex-order1, primary-muscle-group chest, day-order do1 AND
    there is an exercise slot s2 with exercise-order ex-order2, unassigned primary-muscle-group, do2 AND
    do1 and do2 is the same, AND
    ex-order2 minus ex-order1 is 1
THEN 
    Modify s2's primary-muscle-group to be back
