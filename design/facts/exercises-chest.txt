# Chest Exercises

Dumbbell Bench Press primarily targets chest.
Dumbbell Bench Press primarily sub-muscle group is middle chest.
Dumbbell Bench Press secondarily targets shoulder.
Dumbbell Bench Press secondarily targets triceps.
Dumbbell Bench Press is a compound exercise.
Dumbbell Bench Press is a free weight exercise.

Pec Deck primarily targets chest.
Pec Deck primarily sub-muscle group is middle chest.
Pec Deck is an isolation exercise.
Pec Deck is a machine exercise.

Incline Dumbbell Bench Press primarily targets chest.
Incline Dumbbell Bench Press primarily sub-muscle group is upper chest.
Incline Dumbbell Bench Press secondarily targets shoulder.
Incline Dumbbell Bench Press secondarily targets triceps.
Incline Dumbbell Bench Press is a compound exercise.
Incline Dumbbell Bench Press is a free weight exercise.

Low-to-High Cable Chest Fly primarily targets chest.
Low-to-High Cable Chest Fly primarily sub-muscle group is upper chest.
Low-to-High Cable Chest Fly is an isolation exercise.
Low-to-High Cable Chest Fly is a machine exercise.

Chest Dip primarily targets chest.
Chest Dip primarily sub-muscle group is lower chest.
Chest Dip secondarily targets shoulder.
Chest Dip secondarily targets triceps.
Chest Dip is a compound exercise.
Chest Dip is a free weight exercise.

High-to-Low Cable Chest Fly primarily targets chest.
High-to-Low Cable Chest Fly primarily sub-muscle group is lower chest.
High-to-Low Cable Chest Fly is an isolation exercise.
High-to-Low Cable Chest Fly is a machine exercise.
