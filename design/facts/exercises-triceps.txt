# Triceps Exercises

Triceps Pushdown (Bar) primarily targets triceps.
Triceps Pushdown (Bar) primarily sub-muscle group is lateral and medial head.
Triceps Pushdown (Bar) is an isolation exercise.
Triceps Pushdown (Bar) is a machine exercise.

Single-Arm Triceps Extension primarily targets triceps.
Single-Arm Triceps Extension primarily sub-muscle group is lateral and medial head.
Single-Arm Triceps Extension is an isolation exercise.
Single-Arm Triceps Extension is a machine exercise.

Overhead Triceps Extension primarily targets triceps.
Overhead Triceps Extension primarily sub-muscle group is long head.
Overhead Triceps Extension is an isolation exercise.
Overhead Triceps Extension is a free weight exercise.

JM Press primarily targets triceps.
JM Press primarily sub-muscle group is long head.
JM Press is a compound exercise.
JM Press is a free weight exercise.
