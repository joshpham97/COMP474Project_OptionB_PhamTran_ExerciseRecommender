# Hamstring Exercises

Romanian Deadlift primarily targets hamstrings.
Romanian Deadlift primarily sub-muscle group is hamstring (lengthened).
Romanian Deadlift secondarily targets glutes.
Romanian Deadlift is a compound exercise.
Romanian Deadlift is a free weight exercise.

Seated Hamstring Curl primarily targets hamstrings.
Seated Hamstring Curl primarily sub-muscle group is hamstring (shortened).
Seated Hamstring Curl secondarily targets calves.
Seated Hamstring Curl is an isolation exercise.
Seated Hamstring Curl is a machine exercise.
