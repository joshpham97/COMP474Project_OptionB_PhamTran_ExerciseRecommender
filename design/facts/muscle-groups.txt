# Muscle groups

Chest has priority high.
Back has priority high.
Shoulder has priority high.
Quads has priority high.
Hamstring has priority high.
Glutes has priority high.
Biceps has priority low.
Triceps has priority low.
Calves has priority low.
Abs has priority low.

# Muscle group properties

## An exercise can be upper or lower body

Chest exercises are upper body.
Back exercises are upper body.
Biceps exercises are upper body.
Triceps exercises are upper body.
Shoulder exercises are upper body.
Hamstring exercises are lower body.
Quads exercises are lower body.
Glutes exercises are lower body.
Calves exercises are lower body.

## An exercise can be push, pull or leg

Chest exercises are push exercises.
Triceps exercises are push exercises.
Shoulder exercises are push exercises.
Back exercises are pull exercises.
Biceps exercises are pull exercises.
Hamstring exercises are legs exercise.
Quads exercises are legs exercise.
Glutes exercises are legs exercise.
Calves exercises are legs exercise.
